Noah's Ark

Noah’s Ark Shelter is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and abused animals. We provide medical care, temporary shelter, and advocate for responsible pet ownership through education and outreach. Our mission is driven by compassion and commitment, aiming to give every animal a second chance at a safe, loving, and permanent home.

Our Mission

Get Involved

Join us in making a difference for animals in need! Whether you choose to volunteer your time, open your home to a rescued pet through adoption, or contribute through donations, your support helps provide food, medical care, and shelter for abandoned and homeless animals. Every act of kindness brings them closer to a loving forever home.
